Our Education

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide exemplary academic support programs and services that enrich the learning experience of students within an engaging and welcoming environment.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to support our students in their efforts to achieve their academic goals, develop into self-regulated learners, and thrive within Singapore's academically challenging environment.

  • We care

    Education and development are important, but we know that nothing matters more than the safety, security and health of your child. That’s why protection and preparedness are critical considerations in everything we do.

Our Service Features

• Lessons are conducted in small class size so that teachers can optimize personalized attention for individual students.

• Step-by-step approach with examples, classwork practice and homework assignment.

• Specially designed worksheets with comprehensive notes in line with current moe syllabus.

• Structured revision before examinations.

• Past-years school examination papers discussion.

• Regular term tests & semestral assessments.

• Individualized study plan for students taking psle,’n’.’o’.’a’ level examinations.

• Regular progress feedback.

Teacher Quality Service

• Be at least 10 minutes early to get ready for class.

• Start and end lesson punctually.

• Prepare all worksheets and lesson plan at least a lesson ahead. Record all assignments given and test results.

• Ensure that all students complete assignments and corrections. All work as well as corrections must be marked.

• Try your best to give attention to all individuals in the class and understand their learning habits and difficulties so that we can help them to improve in their grades.

• Regular feedback to the parents on their children’s performance is encouraged.

• Mark attendance every lesson and inform us of any absenteeism.

• Enforce student behaviour code of conducts.

• Report to the manager should any emergencies or complaints arises at the centre.
